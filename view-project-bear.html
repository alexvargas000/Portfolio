Mei (Original Short Film)

Mei, a young and careless girl, spots something incredible during a midnight skateboarding sesh. Her curiosity gets the better of her when she decides to investigate and ends up discovering something unimaginable.

The Idea

Being inspired by films such as Coraline, I went into the project with the idea of attempting to make a stop-motion style short with my favorite aesthetics. As such, the animation is animated at 12fps to replicate stop-motion's choppy motion.

The style and color palette of the film came to me after being inspired by @natumi_color on instagram and seing some screenshots of "The Night is Short, Walk on Girl" (Pictures shown above).

My goal with this film is both to improve my animation skills and create something that's soothing to watch and lets a viewer feel both relaxed and engaged with the story.

The Plot

Mei, after deciding to skateboard at midnight to cool off her head, ends up witnessing an UFO crash. With her small town mostly being unaware of this, it's up to her to investigate just what happened and who, if anyone, just landed on her planet.

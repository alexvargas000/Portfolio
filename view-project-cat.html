Ghost Cat

A floating ghost cat getting into the fall mood by indulging in his guilty pleasure of pumpkin spice lattes :)

The Idea

This was made as part of my "100 Days" series, where I created an unique piece of 3D art every day for 100 days. This was day 54 and it was during October, the peak month for pumpkin spice lattes, a drink which I'm extremely fond of. I came up with the idea after seeing an image of casper the ghost and thinking I wanted to make something similar to him but cuter and less uncanny-valley. This led me to make a cat ghost instead of a humanoid one, and the pumpkin spice latte prop came to me naturally.

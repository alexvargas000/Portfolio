BMO - Rises the Moon

I heard this version of Rises the Moon and as a huge Adventure Time fan I immediately started working on this. The song's comforting and almost sleepy tune was a perfect mix with the character known as BMO. I'm really happy how it came out and think it matches the song quite well.

Models and animation made in Blender
Textured in Substance Paint

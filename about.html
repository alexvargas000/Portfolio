Hi! I’m Alex Vargas and I’m based in New York City :)

Digital media has always been my biggest source of entertainment growing up as a homebody. Now, it is what I specialize in. I focus primarily on 3D art and animation (I absolutely love animated films), video editing. I also have a very strong passion for creating interactive experiences through coding.

I am inspired by filmmakers such as Wong Kar Wai, Wes Anderson, and Laika Studios. Video games like Hollow Knight and Splatoon also serve as a huge inspiration for the type of interactive experiences I want to create.

I want to become a pioneer that explores new ways to elevate video game visuals and mechanics as well as 3D animation, allowing them to be appreciated by even wider audiences.

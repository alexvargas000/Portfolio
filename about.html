Hi! I’m Alex Vargas and I’m based in New York City :)

Growing up as a homebody, digital media was my greatest source of entertainment and as such became my passion. I now focus primarily on 3D art and animation (I absolutely love animated films), video editing, and have a very strong passion for coding and creating interactive experiences.

Some of my biggest inspirations come in the form of filmmakers and their work such as Wong Kar-Wai, Wes Anderson, and Laika Studios. Video games like Hollow Knight and Splatoon also serve as a huge inspiration for the type of interactive experiences I want to create.

My main goal as an artist is to push myself and the boundaries of the mediums I work in. I want to bridge the gap between video games and art to the mainstream audience and to push what 3D animated films can achieve.

Alex Vargas is a New York City-based self-taught artist.

Primarily focusing in 3D art, he's modeled over 110 pieces of art, also creating several mini-animations, one short film titled "Raccook", and is currently focused on another with the working title of "Mei".

Also interested in other digital art mediums, such as video games, he's learned how to code in various languages including C# and JavaScript.

Some of his biggest inspirations take the form of movies, video games and digital artists including Coraline, Spider-Man: Into the Spiderverse, and Kirby: the Forgotten Land.
